Overall Outcomes

First, we'll take a look at your overall outcomes. The plot on the right contains the outcome proportions. Red is bad, Orange may be ok depending on the transfer, and Blue and Green are good!

Although our mission is to save every single animal, we should always set intermediate goals and track our progress towards those goals. No Kill defines the target as 90% survival, but we think 95% or better is a great goal to aim for.

The following sections will help you break down these results so you can understand how to boost this number.

Overall Population

These are your overall population numbers.

It is often helpful to think about what segments of this population you are serving both as a proportion of your whole population and individually.

For instance, you may be serving moms and pups very well, but they may account for a relatively small proportion of your population; while large dogs may be less well served and make up a larger portion. This would indicate that a focus on large dog programs would be called for.
